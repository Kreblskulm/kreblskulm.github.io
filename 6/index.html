A (little) change to my blog posts - 31/10/21

I have decided to do a little experimenting when it comes to my website, my little bubble on the internet and the blog posts I am currently writing. Starting from now, any blog posts I release will now come with an accompanying video for my new YouTube channel. Any blog post released will now just be the published scripts for my videos, so if people do prefer to read my content, they will be able to do so. By doing this I am hoping to reach a new audience of people who are more interested in watching videos rather than reading blog posts. I hope by taking this step I am able to get more people interested in open source software like I am.
